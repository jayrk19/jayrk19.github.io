ERP Implementation and Services

Enterprise resource planning (ERP) is a commercial software system which integrates the core processes and the main administration function. Enterprise Resource Planning enhances productivity, flexibility and customer responsiveness, Eliminate Costs and inefficiencies, extends the scope of business expansions and enables easy access to data anywhere and anytime. Revuesofts ERP products, Race and CampuSmart ,are end to end Automation solutions suitable for SMEs and Educational Institutions respectively. The ERPs being web based are easily accessible from anywhere and anytime and completely customisable. Owing to their robust and smart functionalities, they are simple to implement and easy to use. Revuesoft also provides post implementation ERP maintenance and support, which unlike in other ERPs, is cost effective and hassle free.
