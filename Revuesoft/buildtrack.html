BuildTrack

BuildTrack , Revuesoft's flagship Mobile App, BuildTrack is a smart Mobile app which is developed to track, manage and monitor the Infrastructure projects under regular and the Public private partnership mode. BuildTrack covers the entire Life Cycle of the Infrastructure Projects starting from the Tenders stage till the project completion and maintenance. The updated status information of the projects can be viewed anytime as the information will be updated periodically from the project location by the users.
