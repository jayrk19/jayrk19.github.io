NPA Tracker App

NPA Mobile Tracker App is a mobile application, used to track the loans and advances released by the bank through its branches across the country. The NPA tracker mobile app accelerates the process to update, track and monitor the loan portfolio of the all the NPA accounts in the entire Bank. It facilitates the bank officials to view all the relevant loans with complete details, assign/reassign the loans accounts for a more closer followup, update follow up calls and field visit details, update images etc. It is a smart NPA management app well equipped with various features like, SMS/Calling/mailing the staff/ borrowers and also Location tagging facility. NPA Tracker to be installed on mobile phones and tablets, ensures real time tracking and monitoring of the NPA.
