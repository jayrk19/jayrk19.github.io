Race ERP

Race ERP is a complete end to end ERP solution targeted at Small and Mid-sized businesses and large enterprises. It automates both the front and the back-office processes and is easy to implement and also easy to use. The software being web based connects every user to the web through their smart devices, thus enabling easy accessibility and anytime anywhere data management. Race ERP is a completely integrated and customized multi-user multi-location and Multi Currency supporting ERP system for meeting the information needs of any organisation across the Globe. It has 16 modules, including core modules like Accounts, HR, CRM, Inventory, E-Commerce etc. Race ERP can be configured suitably and implemented quickly, which in turn enhances the Quality of service and improves employee performance and productivity which directly contributes to the growth and development of the organisation.
